Theme of the Symposium:
A critical feature of successful product development is the judicious selection of the best material, based on an informed awareness of its capabilities and coupled with manufacturing that takes full advantage of those capabilities. Materials characterization is an important area of fundamental and technological interest, without which no scientific understanding of engineering materials capability could be ascertained. Characterisation in manufacturing, ranges over various length scales from atomistic or nano-scale to macroscopic dimensions. The objective of the symposium is to create a cross disciplinary summit that lends itself to the integration of research and education in the vital field of processing/manufacturing and characterization of materials.
